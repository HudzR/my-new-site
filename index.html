Here is how we work with different types of walls:

Drywall

Drywall itself is fragile, which is why we never rely only on it when mounting heavy panels. We use professional detectors to locate studs behind the drywall sheet. Fastening directly to the wooden or metal frame guarantees maximum strength, even for large diagonals.

Tile

Working with tile requires jeweler-like precision. We use special diamond drill bits that go through the tile without chipping or cracking. Your expensive renovation will remain undamaged.

Important: Where possible, we locate studs behind the tile. This allows the TV to be anchored into the frame, relieving stress from the ceramic itself.

Concrete Wall

This is the most reliable basis for mounting. However, concrete requires professional tools (a hammer drill or perforator) and high-quality anchors. We guarantee that a TV of any diagonal will be held "rock-solid."

Brick Wall

For reliable fastening, we use high-precision professional tools to prevent damage to the brick. We always select special reinforced anchors and wall plugs that are designed for the specific type of masonry, guaranteeing that your TV is secured as safely and reliably as possible.

Perfect Mount for Every Need

Types of TV Mounts

Choose the perfect mount for your TV and viewing needs:

Slim Fit (Low-Profile)

The ideal solution for minimalism and aesthetics. Mounts the TV maximally close to the wall (creating a picture effect, minimal gap), best suited for modern thin OLED/QLED models.

Fixed

Classic reliability and stability. Fixes the TV directly to the wall, ensuring maximum stability and a clean, stationary look. The best choice if the viewing angle does not change.

Tilting

Provides the ability for vertical TV tilt. Ideal for mounting that is slightly above eye level, helping to eliminate glare and improve visibility.

Full-Motion

Maximum flexibility and functionality. Allows extending, tilting, and rotating the TV in any direction to optimize the viewing angle from different points in the room.

Pull-Down (Fireplace Lift)

An ergonomic solution for high mounting. This mount allows the TV (e.g., placed over a fireplace) to be easily lowered to eye level for comfortable viewing and avoiding neck strain.
